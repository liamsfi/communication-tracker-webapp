Communication Tracker

This lightweight web app allows you to maintain a structured log of your communication updates. Use the form below to add new entries. Your data is saved in your browser's local storage so it persists across sessions on the same computer.

Bulk Import from Excel

Copy rows from your Excel spreadsheet containing columns in the order: Date, User, Category, Description/Update (additional columns will be ignored). Then click inside the area below and paste your table (Ctrl+V or Command+V). The table will appear with its formatting, and the data will be added to the log automatically. Header rows (with column names) are skipped.
